The Waliki Friends network is comprised of UCB students who have participated in the Student Mobility Program abroad, who, under the values of solidarity and reciprocity, offer support to students coming to Bolivia from abroad.

The Waliki Friends network, comprised of UCB students who have participated in the Student Mobility Program abroad, is guided by the values of solidarity and reciprocity to support incoming international students.

Students will have the chance to learn about the kallawayas, itinerant traditional healers in Bolivia who are well-known for their deep understanding of medicinal plants and ancient healing practices. Through this encounter, students can explore Andean traditional medicine, gaining a deeper appreciation for the connections between health, culture, and spirituality, and recognizing the continued significance of these practices in many Bolivian communities.

Students will meet and learn about the kallawayas, traditional healers in Bolivia who use plants to heal people. This will help students understand Andean medicine and how it's still important to many communities in Bolivia.
